About us

We are "The Pseudo Dopamine Centre" (Or TPDC for short) - A Discord server made for like-minded people to hang out and enjoy some chill vibes with each other.

At TPDC, we're always looking for opportunities that allow us, as a community, to come together and create something amazing for everyone to share, in addition to benefitting the wider world around us. Since the server's creation in 2019, the server has grown past 100 memebers and has raised £170 for COVID-19 relief.

Whether you're looking to join us in our cause, or you're just looking for a new place to unwind, please feel free to join us! :D
